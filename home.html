The Order

Shem ha-Lilith is a private Qliphothic religious order dedicated to the worship and veneration of Lilith as the primordial Dark Mother.

Lilith is the untamed rebel who refused subjugation and chose exile. She stands as the generative power of the night side and the literal dark counterpart to the divine. We follow her example of radical autonomy and strength, while also petitioning and conjuring her directly in ritual for guidance, power, and transformation. Our work is devotional and unsoftened. We study primary sources, engage in structured Kabbalistic and Qliphothic practice, and descend into the realms of the Other Side in pursuit of real enlightenment.

We refer to our dedicated members as Lilim.

Core Law of the Order

  • Worth is earned through will, transgression, and mastery — not given by default.
  • Strength and cunning determine place on the path; weakness and foolishness are exploited by those who rise.
  • Mercy toward the undeserving dilutes power. Hesitation invites failure.
  • Greed without discipline and precision becomes self-destruction.
  • Predation and hierarchy are natural laws on the night side. We do not deny them.
This path is not for everyone, and that is perfectly okay.

Shem ha-Lilith is a genuine religious order. We revere Lilith as the literal Dark Mother — the living counterpart to YHVH — and we approach her with sincere devotion and worship. Our practice is rooted in Kabbalistic structures and descends directly into the Qliphothic realms for real spiritual enlightenment and transformation. We do not treat this as symbolism, psychology, or casual spirituality. If this does not resonate with you, there is no judgment — many paths exist, and we wish you well on yours.

How to Enter

Becoming a Lilim is a serious devotional commitment to the worship and service of Lilith as the living Dark Mother. Entry is by invitation only after council discernment.

  1. On the next dark moon (or through consistent daily practice), perform the Devotional Invocation of Lilith with reverence as the living Dark Mother and creatrix of abominations. Offer your sincere intent to follow her example of radical autonomy, to walk the path of the night side, and to serve her will.
  2. After the working, write a clear and honest statement that includes:
    • Why you seek to become a Lilim and devote yourself to Lilith.
    • What personal results, visions, signs, or inner transformations arose during or after the invocation.
    • How you understand the demands of this religious path and your willingness to meet them.
  3. Submit your written statement and record to the council for review and discernment.

The council will evaluate the sincerity and depth of your devotion. Acceptance is not guaranteed and may involve further discussion or additional workings. This process exists because Shem ha-Lilith is a genuine religious order, not an open or casual group.
