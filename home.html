The Dark Mother

This is the private living archive of a serious Qliphothic order dedicated to Lilith as the primordial Mother of Demons and Abominations.

We work with her ancient form from the Burney Relief — the Night Hag, creatrix of the Sitra Ahra, who births lilim, succubi, incubi and all abominations from the void.

Who This Order Is For

  • Practitioners who treat Lilith as living reality, not symbol or meme
  • Those ready for real ritual work, study, and dark moon practice
  • Seekers who accept the raw law of the Other Side without softening it
This path is not for everyone. Mockery, spectacle, edge-lord behavior, or atheist materialist attitudes will get you removed. The current itself filters the unworthy.

How to Begin

  1. Perform the solitary Burney Relief invocation on the next dark moon.
  2. Document any vision, sign, or personal abomination that arises.
  3. Submit your experience to the council for review.

Entry is invitation-only after the initiation is accepted.
