The Blasphemous Name • The Path of Exile

The Order

This is the private living archive of a Qliphothic order dedicated to Lilith as the primordial Dark Mother and creatrix of abominations.

She is the untamed force who refused subjugation, the rebel who chose exile over obedience, and the generative power of the night side. She births the lilim and all abominations from the void, filling the Sitra Ahra with living chaos and raw potential. We work with her current through direct ritual, study of primary sources, and honest personal practice — without softening or sanitizing the work.

Core Law of the Order

  • Not everyone is equal. Worth is chosen through will, transgression, and mastery.
  • The strong exploit the stupid. The willful crush the weak.
  • Mercy is dilution. Hesitation is death.
  • Open greed without precision is self-destruction.
  • Predation is natural law in the Other Side.
This path is not for everyone, and that is perfectly okay.

It requires a sincere commitment to working with Lilith as a living force and engaging in real ritual practice. Those who approach the work with mockery or a desire for casual entertainment are not a good fit and will be asked to step away.

How to Enter

  1. Perform a solitary invocation of Lilith on the next dark moon, approaching her as the living Dark Mother and creatrix of abominations.
  2. Document any vision, sign, or personal result that arises.
  3. Submit your record to the council for review.

Entry is by invitation only after the initiation is accepted. This path remains private and initiatory.
