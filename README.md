# Shem ha-Lilith

**The Blasphemous Name • The Path of Exile**

This repository holds the living archive of a private Qliphothic order dedicated to Lilith — not as a reclaimed goddess or modern archetype, but as the primordial Night Hag, devourer of the weak, and Dark Feminine counterpart to YHVH.

We invert the divine structures without apology. We corrupt the Shem. We walk the Sitra Ahra and embrace the raw law of the husks.

### Core Law of the Order
- Not everyone is equal. Worth is chosen through will, transgression, and mastery.
- The strong exploit the stupid. The willful crush the weak.
- Mercy is dilution. Hesitation is death.
- Open greed without precision is self-destruction.
- Predation is natural law in the Other Side.

Lilith demands authenticity, dread, and unsoftened devotion. There is no equality here, no performative inclusion, and no mercy for the weak or the complacent.

This space is for those who have passed the gates and chosen ascent. All others remain outside.

Enter at your own risk.
